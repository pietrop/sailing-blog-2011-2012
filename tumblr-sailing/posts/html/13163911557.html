The 4 stages of learning

Back at UKSA to change boat and skipper…

Yesterday our skip has told us about the four stages of learning, in relation to the learning curve in sailing

1-Unconscious Incompetence
ie his 4 years old son going behind the wheel of the car thinking he’ll know what to do.. But then realising he knows nothing about it ..

2- Conscious Incompetence

Understanding the limits of not knowing a certain skill or task and the value of learning it…

3- Conscious Competence

Executing the skill/task still requires concentration to perform

4- Unconscious Competence
Skill mastered and can be performed while multi-tasking

[ http://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Four_stages_of_competence ]

I think there’s definitely some truth there and I can see examples of where that might work but not completely sold on it..seems a bit too simplistic..
